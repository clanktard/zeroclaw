=== ANIMATION CURRICULUM ===
Source: pixelengine.ai/guides/animate
Lesson 1: Prompts must be temporally dense + motion-focused
Lesson 2: Starting pose is part of the prompt
Lesson 3: Give characters canvas space to move
Lesson 4: Frame counts matter (attacks 8-12, idle 4-8, walk 6-8)
Lesson 5: 24 colors is the default sweet spot
Lesson 6: Re-rolling 3-4 times is normal
